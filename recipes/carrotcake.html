Carrot cake from Heaven

This is so far the best carrot cake recipes I have ever tested

The fisrt time I tried this recipe was at a school party, my english teacher made it, and since then it became my favorite cake

In my memory of miss hailey

Ingredients

  • Grated carrots
  • vegetable oil
  • 3 Eggs
  • Vanilla extract
  • Baking soda
  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Pinch of salt

Steps

  1. Mix the dry ingredients together
  2. Mix eggs with oil
  3. Add the dry ingredients and mix well
  4. Cook in a heated oven for 40 minutes
  5. Let it cool before serving
